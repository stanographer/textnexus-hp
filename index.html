    Who We Are

    We are a professional, creative team of live captioners with multiple years of experience working with a broad range of events of every type and scale. Our passion is providing accessibility through live captioning in a manner that fits both our clients' branding and aesthetics, while managing the behind-the-scenes details so you don’t have to worry about them.

    Our goal is to provide our clients the highest caliber of service to expand ADA accommodations through delivering accurate captions, all while using the latest technology to do so. Our objective is to make your event a leader in the industry by providing and delivering captions that not only are customized to your event, but also make it more accessible and beneficial to everyone.

    Accessibility Strategist, Live Stenographer

    As the majority of Isaiah's experience in stenography comes from that of working in the legal field as an Official Court Reporter, accuracy is of the utmost importance.

    Whether it be providing live realtime of the proceedings during trials, or providing captions for ADA compliance to hard-of-hearing individuals in the courtroom, technology is always at the forefront.
